AJANI GOLDMANE

Duty, honor, and valor are either in your heart or they are not. You will never know for certain until you are tested

Ajani is a leonin warrior who aids his allies with potent healing and protective powers. Known across the Multiverse as a noble hero and compassionate friend, Ajani devotes himself to protecting the weak and fighting against those who seek to oppress others. As a member of the Gatewatch, Ajani often provides mentorship to his fellow Planeswalkers, seeking to help them master their gifts and grow into their best selves.
